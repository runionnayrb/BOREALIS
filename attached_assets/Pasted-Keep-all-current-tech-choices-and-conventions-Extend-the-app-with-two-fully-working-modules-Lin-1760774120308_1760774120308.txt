New goals at a glance

Lineup Builder is a visual drag and drop editor to design the canonical lineup template. It is opened from a button inside the Daily Lineup page, not as a top level route.

Daily Lineup is the show day tool that instantiates a template for a specific date and show number, validates eligibility, and publishes to PDF and a digital lineup screen.

Performance Wellness owns restrictions. Coaching owns certain positions and creates integration plans. Stage Management runs the daily assignment workflow.

Distinguished Artists controls day of OUT, BACK IN, and Long Term Out states. OUT and Long Term Out artists must never appear as eligible.

Daily Lineup shows scenes as tabs, prints with four scenes per page on A3 and A4, and provides a read only high contrast digital display route, plus a mobile friendly view.

All configuration is done in the UI. No engineer is required for routine changes.

Domain model to infer and implement
Design normalized relational schemas that satisfy these behaviors.

A. Lineup templates
• A template contains ordered scenes. Each scene contains free positioned Position blocks laid out on a canvas.
• Each Position has label, department, optional maximum assignees, required competencies, optional tags, an ownership flag of Stage Management or Coaching, style controls, and a layout rectangle.
• Version the template so editors can save V1, V1.1, and similar.

B. Competencies and tasks
• Competencies belong to departments and can require tasks.
• Tasks include title, detail, evidence type, and a required signoff role such as Coach, SM, Physio, Rigging, or Other.

C. Integration plans and reintegration plans
• Integration Plan templates define steps an artist must complete to enter a cue, act, or position. Steps have order, title, detail, required flag, and signoff role. A plan can link to a scene, act, or position.
• Re integration Plan is per artist for return after a long absence. Steps mirror the template behavior but are specific to that artist.

D. Distinguished Artists
• Status values: OUT, BACK IN, LONG TERM OUT, with reason, effective from, optional expected return.
• If OUT or LONG TERM OUT is active, the artist must be hidden from position pickers and must be ineligible for assignment.

E. Performance Wellness restrictions
• A restriction has category like medical, coaching, rigging, physio, other, severity of hard or soft, optional scope across scenes, acts, positions, or departments, notes, and effective dates.
• Hard restriction blocks assignment. Soft restriction is a warning.
• Restrictions originate from the Performance Wellness side, but are readable in Lineup and used for eligibility.

F. Daily Lineup instance
• A Daily Lineup is created from a template for a specific date and show number.
• It stores day specific outs, notes, assignments, warnings, and publish time.
• Assignments link a position to an artist with validation state, reasons, and optional justification for overrides.

G. Print and display presets
• Create presets for four scenes per page on A3 and A4 with margins, gutter, header and footer logos, and font scale.
• Create a digital display preset for read only high contrast screens that auto refresh and can rotate scenes.

Eligibility service rules
Run checks in this order, return result pass, warn, or fail and an array of reasons.

Distinguished Artists. If OUT or LONG TERM OUT is active, fail and hide from pickers.

Performance Wellness restrictions. If a hard restriction applies to this position by scope, fail with reason. If a soft restriction applies, warn with reason.

Ownership and access. In the Coaching application scope, positions owned by Stage Management are view only. Stage Management can assign anywhere.

Competencies and required tasks. If required competencies or tasks are incomplete, fail. If an Integration Plan is linked and required steps are incomplete, warn or fail based on plan settings.

Re integration plan. If the artist has an active plan with steps that constrain this position, warn or fail as configured.

Capacity and duplicates. Enforce maximum assignees and flag double booking.
Support an override pathway for Stage Management with required justification on the assignment and an audit trail. Provide a separate permission for overriding a hard Performance Wellness restriction, default off.
